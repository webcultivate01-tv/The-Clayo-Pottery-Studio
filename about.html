Our Journey

12 Years of Transforming Skin & Lives

Founded in 2012 by Dr. Meera Joshi, Lumière Skin Clinic was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves healthy, glowing skin regardless of their skin type, tone, or concern. What began as a small dermatology practice has evolved into a full-service skin center equipped with state-of-the-art technology.

We blend the precision of modern dermatology with a deep respect for each patient's individuality. No two skin journeys are the same, and neither are our treatment plans. We listen first, treat second.

01
Patient First, Always

Your comfort, safety, and results are our only priority. We never rush — we listen, understand, and then act with precision.

02
Evidence-Based Medicine

Every treatment is clinically proven, peer-reviewed, and science-backed. We do not experiment — we transform with confidence.

03
Personalized Skin Care

Custom plans crafted for your unique skin type, Fitzpatrick tone, lifestyle, diet, and long-term skin health goals.

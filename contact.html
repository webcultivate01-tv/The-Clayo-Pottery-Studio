Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

We recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot. However, we do accommodate walk-ins based on daily availability. Call us first to check availability before walking in.
Yes! Your first skin analysis consultation with our dermatologist is completely free of charge. During this session, we'll assess your skin type, Fitzpatrick tone, current concerns, and recommend the most suitable, cost-effective treatment plan tailored specifically for you.
The number of sessions varies based on your skin concern and chosen treatment. For example, laser resurfacing typically requires 3–6 sessions spaced 4 weeks apart, while HydraFacial results are visible after a single session. Our dermatologist will give you a realistic treatment timeline during your free consultation.
Absolutely. We specialize in treating Fitzpatrick types III–VI skin tones, which are most common in Indian patients. We use FDA-approved devices and conduct mandatory patch tests before any laser or chemical procedure. Our doctors have extensive experience with Indian skin and understand the unique risks such as post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation.
Downtime varies by treatment. Non-invasive treatments like HydraFacial and Oxygen Facial have zero downtime — you can return to daily activities immediately. Chemical peels may involve mild peeling for 3–5 days. Fractional laser may require 5–7 days of recovery. We'll explain detailed post-treatment care protocols for every procedure.
Yes! We offer 0% interest EMI options on selected treatment packages for up to 6 months through our banking partners. We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash. Ask our front desk team about current EMI plans and package discounts during your consultation.
General preparation includes avoiding direct sun exposure for 48–72 hours, not waxing or bleaching the area 1 week prior, removing makeup before your appointment, and staying hydrated. Specific preparations vary by treatment — we will send you detailed pre-care instructions via WhatsApp after booking your appointment.
We treat both men and women with equal expertise. Male skin is generally thicker with larger pores and higher sebum production, so treatment protocols are adjusted accordingly. Results are comparable across genders — our dermatologists will always customize the approach for your specific skin biology and goals.
